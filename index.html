Introduction

Fun fact: I started coding using the Dutch version of Scratch

My name is Rosalie Wessels. I was born in the Netherlands in May 2005, and I started coding at 9 years old (my Scratch account was created beginning of 2015). I have since moved to the United States in December 2016, and attended an American middle and high school. I also continued programming, expanding to languages like SwiftUI & Swift (but also UIKit), HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python.

May 2020 - TBD
