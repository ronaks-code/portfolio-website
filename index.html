Technovation



I competed in Technovation for two years.

The first year I participated together with Julia and Mahika (my friends from Egan at the time). We built an app called Studify, which would motivate students to do their homework by turning it into a competition. We learned Swift together, meeting every Saturday afternoon at the library or in my dad's office. After months, we built a fully functional app, with integration with Google Firebase and the Google Classroom API. We also created a pitch video and demo video, and finally pitched the app live during the regionals day in the Salesforce Tower in San Francisco in front of a panel of judges. Our pitch was a huge success, and we ended up winning that round and progressing to the worldwide semifinals! All the pictures from above are from that year and showcase the amazing experience.

Mahika and I participated again the following year, with an updated (and better) version called Productiv8. However, it was most likely too similar to the previous year, so we did not make it to the semifinals that year.

Technical skills learned:

  • Swift + UIKit
  • Working with Firbase
  • Working with the Google Classroom API
  • Github/Source Control

Soft skills learned:

  • Collaboration
  • Presenting
